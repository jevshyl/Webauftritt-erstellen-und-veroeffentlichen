Welcome to my Page where I will be showing you how to make a Cheese Cake.
But first of all, I've been making this Cheese Cake version for years now. I've started liking it for the first time when my father had made it with me when I was 8 years old. I liked it so much that I decided to try and learn how to make one myself. Since then I've tried to recreate it and used different recipes for it. The recipe that I will be showing you is one of my favorite ones. Its sweet but also a little salty. It doesnt require too many ingredients and is quite easy to replicate. The reason you should try and bake it is clearly for the taste but also the fun experiece you might have. I've baked it so many times with so many different people, which all loved it.

Ingredients for 8 people

Name Amount
Wholemeal butter biscuits 200g
Butter 50g
Salt 2 pinches
Philadelphia 400g
Sugar 100g
Vanilla Stick 1
Crème fraîche 200g
Eggs 2

Recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Take the biscuits and put them in a plastc bag. Finely crush them until they look somewhat like that:
  3. Your next step is to take melt the butter slowly in a pan.
  4. Mix the melt butter with the crushed biscuits and the salt.
  5. Prepare your cake form. Take eiher tin foil and cover the form with it, or what also works is taking some butter and spreading it over the bottom and sides of the form.
  6. Take your cake form and press the biscuit-mix onto the bottom. Make sure to press it down well, so that the whole floor is covered.
  7. Take out a bigger bowl that will be used for the next micture
  8. Take the vanilla stick and cut it open lengthwise. Take out the seeds and put them into the bowl.
  9. Add the philadelphie, eggs and sugar to the bowl and mix everything by hand or with a mixer.
  10. Bake the cake for about 30 minutes.
  11. After 30 minutes, take the cake out and let it cool down for about 5 minutes.
  12. Take the Crème fraîche and spread it on the top of the cake.
  13. Bake the cake for another 15 minutes.
  14. After taking the cake out, cover it and let itcool down in your fridge for at least 6 hours.
